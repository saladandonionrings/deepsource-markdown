# Missing whitespace around arithmetic operator
**ID:** `FLK-E226` | **Lien:** [DeepSource](https://deepsource.com/directory/python/issues/FLK-E226)

![Minor](https://img.shields.io/badge/severity-minor-yellow) ![Style](https://img.shields.io/badge/type-style-blue)

There should be one space before and after an arithmetic operator ( `+` , `-` , `/` , and `*` ).

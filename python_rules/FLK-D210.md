# No whitespaces allowed surrounding docstring text
**ID:** `FLK-D210` | **Lien:** [DeepSource](https://deepsource.com/directory/python/issues/FLK-D210)

![Minor](https://img.shields.io/badge/severity-minor-yellow) ![Documentation](https://img.shields.io/badge/type-documentation-lightgrey)

You should avoid spaces at the beginning and at the end of a docstring.

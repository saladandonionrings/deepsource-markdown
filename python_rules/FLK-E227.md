# Missing whitespace around bitwise or shift operator
**ID:** `FLK-E227` | **Lien:** [DeepSource](https://deepsource.com/directory/python/issues/FLK-E227)

![Minor](https://img.shields.io/badge/severity-minor-yellow) ![Style](https://img.shields.io/badge/type-style-blue)

There should be one space before and after bitwise and shift operators ( `<<` , `>>` , `&` , `|` , `^` ).

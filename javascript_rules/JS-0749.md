# Prefer to have a unique title in `<iframe>`
**ID:** `JS-0749` | **Lien:** [DeepSource](https://deepsource.com/directory/javascript/issues/JS-0749)

![Minor](https://img.shields.io/badge/severity-minor-yellow) ![Anti-pattern](https://img.shields.io/badge/type-anti_pattern-purple)

`<iframe>` elements must have a unique title property to indicate its content to the user.


## Bad Practice

```javascript
<iframe />
<iframe {...props} />
<iframe title="" />
<iframe title={''} />
<iframe title={``} />
<iframe title={undefined} />
<iframe title={false} />
<iframe title={true} />
<iframe title={42} />
```

## Recommended

```javascript
<iframe title="This is a unique title" />
<iframe title={uniqueTitle} />
```
